WHY IMPROV?

WHAT IS IMPROV?

Improv (short for improvisation) is a skill practiced by everyone, every single day- it's the practice of unplanned, spontaneous interaction with others. In an improv performance, actors create plot, characters, and dialogue on the spot without the use of a plan or a script. 

IMPROV AT WORK

The skills and processes of improv are very useful in a business context. Improv teaches you to think fast, risk speaking up, and effectively communicate.

YOUR GROUP WILL LEARN HOW TO:

Build confidence in public speaking, presentations, sales pitches, etc.

Promote creative and adaptive problem solving

Foster better communication

Build trust and support in teams

Manage conflict expertly

Enhance focus and concentration

Manage change more effectively

Communicate more effectively in crisis

Overcome blocks to creativity and organisational innovation
