OUR WORKSHOPS

Through an incredibly supporting and fun environment, our workshop will teach your team the core communication foundations of improv, which include listening, awareness, communication, and support.

WORKSHOP FORMAT

Our Corporate Improv Workshops last from 2 to 3 hours and can be arranged for groups of 10 to 20 participants.

No props are required, although we do suggest participants wear comfortable clothing, i.e. no jackets and ties!

To start off, we conduct a series of warm up exercises which get people used to speaking up, playing a character, and using physical gestures to express themselves. Afterwards we conduct ‘games’, which have prescribed rules and are used to enhance specific skills.

A typical warm up exercise is for everyone to stand in a circle and pass an imaginary ball around, complete with dramatic actions and sound effects. This exercise focuses the group’s attention and encourages them to use their imagination.

A typical game is One Word Story. We gather five to 10 people in a circle and create a new story one word at a time. Participants quickly learn that they have to balance their own ideas and expectations with those of the ensemble. No one can control the outcome. And words like “the” or “and” are just as important as “tortoise” or “hare.” Seemingly small contributions matter greatly to the whole.
